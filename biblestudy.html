Bible Study

About

Our Bible Study attempts to address the spiritual needs of participants at all ages and of all abilities. We sing interactive songs that teach the faith while incorporating percussion instruments. We teach the basics of the faith using sign language: the Ten Commandments, the Apostles Creed, and the Lord’s Prayer. Each week we learn a new Bible lesson and relate it to the gospel for that

Sunday. We want all participants to know chiefly that they are loved by God through Jesus Christ his Son. There are two opportunities to attend our Bible Study: Thursdays at Hangtime and Sundays at 9:30 am. Both Bible studies meet at St. Paul’s Lutheran Church.

About SPARC

SPARC is the community name for the 501(c)3 HOPE for Developmental Disabilities.

SPARC began in 2006 and provides programs and support for children and adults with developmental disabilities and their families.
